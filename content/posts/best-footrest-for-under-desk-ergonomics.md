# The 10 Best Footrests for Under Desk Ergonomics: Boost Comfort While Working

If you spend more than four hours a day sitting at a desk, you’ve likely felt the telltale signs of poor ergonomics: aching lower back, swollen ankles, or that restless feeling in your legs. While most people invest heavily in a high-end ergonomic chair, they often forget the "foundation" of their seated posture—their feet.

An ergonomic under-desk footrest isn't just a luxury; for many, it's a physiological necessity. In this guide, we’ll explore why you need one and review the top models available in 2026.

## Why Your Feet Matter in Desk Ergonomics

When you sit, your feet should be flat on a surface to allow your weight to be distributed evenly through your thighs. If your chair is too high and your feet dangle, or if you find yourself crossing your legs or tucking them under your chair, you’re putting unnecessary pressure on your lower back and restricting blood flow.

### Key Benefits of a Footrest:
1. **Reduces Lower Back Pressure**: By elevating your feet, you naturally sit deeper into your chair’s lumbar support.
2. **Improves Circulation**: Keeping your feet elevated and active (with rocking models) prevents blood from pooling in your lower extremities.
3. **Encourages Movement**: Many modern footrests are designed to rock or tilt, encouraging subtle muscle movement throughout the day.

### 2. The Comfort King: Everlasting Comfort Memory Foam Pier

### 3. The Heavy Duty: Kensington SoleMassage

## What to Look for When Buying
- **Adjustability**: Look for height and angle adjustments.
- **Surface Texture**: Do you want a flat surface or massaging bumps?
- **Stability**: Ensure it has a non-slip base so it doesn't slide away on hardwood floors.

## Conclusion
A footrest is the missing piece of the ergonomic puzzle. By supporting your lower limbs, you enable your entire body to sit in a more natural, stress-free position.
